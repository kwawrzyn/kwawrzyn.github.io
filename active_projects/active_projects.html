Models of MRI RF Coils for Safety Evaluations

Summary: I’m developing simulation models of RF coils for the application of MRI-related device safety evaluation and understanding, with the expected outcome of expanding and diversifying the available options available to us. The project involves building different body-only, head-only, and knee-only coils, determining design specifications and methods, quantitative comparisons of different conditions (i.e. shield vs no shield, loading vs unloading, etc...), V&V of coil performance, etc…
